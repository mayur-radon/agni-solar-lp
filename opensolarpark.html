Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Open Access Solar Parks
What is an Open Access Solar Park and how does it work?

An Open Access Solar Park is a large-scale solar power facility where multiple consumers can purchase electricity directly from the solar park through open access regulations. This allows businesses to access clean, renewable energy at competitive rates without installing solar panels on their own premises. The electricity is transmitted through the existing grid infrastructure.

What are the benefits of investing in an Open Access Solar Park?

Key benefits include significant cost savings (20-30% reduction in electricity costs), long-term price stability, achievement of sustainability goals, reduced carbon footprint, no upfront capital investment required, professional maintenance and operation, and the ability to meet corporate social responsibility objectives while enhancing brand reputation.

What is the minimum capacity requirement to participate in Open Access?

The minimum contract demand for Open Access varies by state but is typically 1 MW or higher. However, through our aggregation model, smaller consumers can also participate by pooling their requirements together. We work with businesses of all sizes to find suitable solutions for accessing solar power through our parks.

How long does it take to set up an Open Access agreement?

The complete process typically takes 3-6 months, including regulatory approvals, grid connectivity studies, and commercial agreements. This includes obtaining necessary approvals from state electricity regulatory commissions, executing power purchase agreements, and completing grid synchronization. Our team handles all regulatory procedures to ensure smooth implementation.

What financing options are available for Solar Park investments?

We offer flexible financing models including CAPEX (capital expenditure), OPEX (operational expenditure), Power Purchase Agreements (PPA), and hybrid financing options. Under PPA models, you can access solar power with zero upfront investment and pay only for the electricity consumed at predetermined rates, making it financially attractive for businesses of all sizes.

Frequently Asked Questions❓

Get answers to common questions about commercial solar installations
What is the typical payback period for commercial solar installations?

The payback period typically ranges from 3-5 years depending on your energy consumption, local electricity rates, available subsidies, and system size. With current incentives and accelerated depreciation benefits, most businesses see positive cash flow from year one. Our team provides detailed ROI calculations and financial modeling during the free consultation.

Do you provide financing options for solar installations?

Yes, we offer multiple flexible financing models: CAPEX (outright purchase with tax benefits), OPEX (operational lease model), PPA (Power Purchase Agreement with fixed rates), and hybrid financing options. We work with leading financial institutions to provide competitive rates and zero down payment options.

What maintenance is required for commercial solar systems?

Solar systems require minimal maintenance - primarily regular cleaning and periodic inspections. We provide comprehensive O&M packages including quarterly cleaning, annual health checks, 24/7 remote monitoring, and immediate response to any issues. Our predictive maintenance approach ensures 99%+ uptime throughout the 25+ year system life.

How much roof space is needed for a commercial solar installation?

Typically, you need about 80-100 square feet per kW of solar capacity, depending on panel efficiency and mounting configuration. Our engineers conduct detailed site surveys using drone technology and 3D modeling to optimize space utilization. We also offer ground-mounted and carport solutions for maximum capacity.

What warranties and guarantees do you provide?

We provide industry-leading warranties: 25-year linear performance warranty on solar panels (guaranteeing 80%+ efficiency after 25 years), 10-year comprehensive warranty on inverters, 5-year installation workmanship warranty, and performance guarantees backed by insurance. All components are from Tier-1 manufacturers with global service networks.

How long does the installation process take?

Installation timelines vary by project size: Small commercial (50-200kW): 2-4 weeks, Medium commercial (200kW-1MW): 4-8 weeks, Large industrial (1MW+): 8-16 weeks. This includes design approval, permits, procurement, installation, and grid connection. We ensure minimal disruption to your business operations during installation.
